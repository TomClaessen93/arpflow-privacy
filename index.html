Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What we collect

Nothing. This app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All your progress, settings, and preferences stay on your device.

Local storage

Your game progress (XP, completed levels, streaks, custom sequences) and audio settings (instrument, noise gate, calibration) are saved to your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device. Clearing your app data will erase it.

No analytics or tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics services, tracking pixels, or any third-party scripts. There is no telemetry. We have no idea how you use the app — and we like it that way.

Microphone access

The app requests microphone permission solely for real-time pitch detection. Audio is processed locally on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. You can revoke microphone access at any time through your device settings.

Children

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is nothing to worry about here.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be included in the app. Since we collect no data, changes would only reflect new features or clarifications.
